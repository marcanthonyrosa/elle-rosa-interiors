

# Elle Rosa Interior Design — Single Page Website

## Overview
A striking, modern single-page website for Elle Rosa's interior design consultancy. Warm cream/beige palette with darker gold accents, creating an elegant and contemporary feel.

## Design Direction
- **Color palette**: Light cream/beige background, dark gold (#B8860B-range) accents, warm charcoal for text
- **Typography**: Clean, modern serif for headings (elegant feel), sans-serif for body text
- **Style**: Generous whitespace, large imagery sections, smooth scroll navigation

## Page Sections (top to bottom)

### 1. Navigation Bar
- Fixed top nav with "Elle Rosa" logo/wordmark on the left
- Smooth-scroll links: About, Services, Portfolio, Contact
- Clean, minimal design with gold accent on active/hover states

### 2. Hero Section
- Full-width hero with a bold headline like *"Designing Spaces That Tell Your Story"*
- Brief tagline about Elle Rosa's design philosophy
- "Get in Touch" call-to-action button with gold styling
- Elegant background with subtle texture or placeholder for a feature image

### 3. About Section
- Brief intro to Elle Rosa — her approach, philosophy, and what sets her apart
- Emphasis on her end-to-end process: from concept to completion
- Clean layout with text alongside a placeholder portrait area

### 4. Services Section
- Three service cards highlighting her core offerings:
  - **Layout & Space Planning** — Designing the overall home layout
  - **Interior Design** — Fixtures, furniture, and finishes
  - **Builder Coordination** — Working with builders to bring it all to life
- Each card with an icon, title, and short description

### 5. Portfolio / Gallery Section
- Grid of project placeholder images showcasing work
- Elegant hover effects with project names
- Clean masonry or uniform grid layout

### 6. Testimonials Section
- Client quotes in an elegant carousel or stacked layout
- Placeholder testimonials with warm, trust-building tone

### 7. Contact Section
- Contact form (name, email, message) with gold-accented submit button
- Contact details: email, phone, location
- Invitation to book a consultation

### 8. Footer
- © Elle Rosa branding
- Social media icon links
- Back-to-top option
